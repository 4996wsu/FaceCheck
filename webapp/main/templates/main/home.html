What is FaceCheck?

Taking attendance is typically inconvenient, time-consuming, and repetitive.

FaceCheck evolves the current process by allowing professors to take attendance much faster and with far less human involvement. FaceCheck uses a live video feed to track which students are currently present, on top of detecting when students leave and re-enter class.

This website also offers various helpful statistics for professors, such as how many students exit class at a given time and attendance reports for each student.
